## Scenarios

### [01: Elena's Three-Minute Vetting](01-elenas-three-minute-vetting/01-elenas-three-minute-vetting.md)
**Persona:** Elena the Event Curator ⭐ — fears booking a hype merchant; wants credibility verified fast with a frictionless invite path
**Pages:** Entrance (Home), Selected Works, Speaking & Writing, The Study (Contact)
**User Value:** Certainty in under 3 minutes — FIA/FIAI anchor + key stats, real dated work, stage evidence, one-tap "Invite Rohan to speak"
**Business Value:** 1+ qualified speaking inquiry per quarter — the flywheel's fastest amplifier

---

### [02: Rahul's 55-Second Proof Hunt](02-rahuls-55-second-proof-hunt/02-rahuls-55-second-proof-hunt.md)
**Persona:** Rahul the Recruiting Lead 🚀 — wants shipped work with process/results depth one tap away; allergic to template portfolios
**Pages:** The Archive (index), Archive Case Detail
**User Value:** Verified rare actuarial×AI hybrid within his 55-second budget
**Business Value:** 2+ qualified consulting/career inquiries per quarter

---

### [03: Ananya's Guided Tour](03-ananyas-guided-tour/03-ananyas-guided-tour.md)
**Persona:** Ananya the Aspiring AI Actuary 🌟 — wants a walkable actuary→AI path and a way to connect; fears the path is unreachable
**Pages:** About the Artist, Docent Conversation (overlay)
**User Value:** A role model's human story + a docent that answers her questions and takes her message
**Business Value:** 30% docent engagement + community amplification of "AI Actuary"

---

### [04: Rahul's Wrong Turn](04-rahuls-wrong-turn/04-rahuls-wrong-turn.md)
**Persona:** Rahul the Recruiting Lead (standing in for all) — fears a dead link confirms a stale, abandoned site
**Pages:** 404 / Fallback
**User Value:** Never lost — the gallery voice routes him back to live content in one tap
**Business Value:** Zero credibility leak from legacy URLs of the old site
