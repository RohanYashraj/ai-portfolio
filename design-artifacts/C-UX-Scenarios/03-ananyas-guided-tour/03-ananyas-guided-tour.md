# 03: Ananya's Guided Tour

**Project:** ai-portfolio
**Created:** 2026-07-12
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
Explore Rohan's actuary→AI path with the docent as guide, read the human story, and leave a non-transactional message — proof the path is walkable and the person is reachable.

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** Docent as standalone case study + guide the next AI actuaries
**Objective:** Objective 3 — 30% of visitors interact with the docent · Objective 5 — community amplification of "AI Actuary"

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** Ananya the Aspiring AI Actuary (Tertiary 🌟)
**Situation:** Ananya, 25, actuarial analyst two exams from qualification, self-teaching ML on evenings. A conference talk of Rohan's was shared on her LinkedIn feed; it's 9pm, she's at her desk after study, and she wants to know if this path is real.

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** Proof the actuary→AI path exists and was walked by someone with the credentials she's earning — plus a way to connect.

**Worry:** More hype without substance, or a figure so senior and gatekept that the path feels unreachable.

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Mobile (evening, personal phone)
**Entry:** Taps the site link from a shared conference talk on LinkedIn, lands on the Entrance, and notices the docent's greeting with suggested taps.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
She's read the human actuary→AI story, had the docent answer her real questions (exams, first AI project, papers to start with), and sent a message that didn't feel like an imposition — she leaves with a map and a connection.

**Business Success:**
A docent interaction counted toward the 30% engagement target, a new community amplifier who follows, cites, and shares — and living proof the docent works as the first exhibit.

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1. **About the Artist** — Reads the one-paragraph human story of the actuary→AI journey with the single portrait; the path becomes imaginable
2. **Docent Conversation (overlay)** — Opens the docent from its suggested tap; asks how Rohan moved from exams to AI; gets grounded, CMS-sourced answers with pointers to papers and talks; accepts the docent's offer to take a message and sends a hello ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

- ✅ **Want:** A visible actuary→AI career path; accessible papers & talks; a way to connect
