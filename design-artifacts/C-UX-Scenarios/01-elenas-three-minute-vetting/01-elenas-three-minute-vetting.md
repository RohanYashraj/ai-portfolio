# 01: Elena's Three-Minute Vetting

**Project:** ai-portfolio
**Created:** 2026-07-12
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
Verify Rohan's AI×actuarial credibility and stage evidence, then send a speaking inquiry — all within one vetting session.

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** Convert visits into speaking invitations — every booked talk amplifies the "AI Actuary" engine
**Objective:** Objective 1 — 1+ qualified speaking inquiry per quarter (Trigger Map, Secondary tier, driven by the Primary Engine goal)

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** Elena the Event Curator (Primary ⭐)
**Situation:** Elena, 41, Head of Programming for an insurance/InsurTech conference series. Three slots left, forty names on her list, one afternoon to shortlist. Rohan's name came via a peer recommendation; she gives his site 2–3 minutes.

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** Find self-evident proof he knows both sides and can hold a room, so she can shortlist him with confidence.

**Worry:** Booking another hype merchant whose "AI expertise" embarrasses her audience — or wasting her afternoon digging for credentials.

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Desktop (office vetting session; the mobile experience must still support her venue-lobby tablet checks and screenshots)
**Entry:** Googles "Rohan Gupta actuary AI" from her office desktop after a peer recommendation, clicks the top result, lands on the Entrance.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
In under 3 minutes she has verified FIA/FIAI + AI depth, seen evidence of past stages with event context, screenshotted credibility signals for her committee, and sent an invite without hunting for an email address.

**Business Success:**
A qualified speaking inquiry captured via the "Invite Rohan to speak" path — measurable against the 1+/quarter target — plus one more curator who can state the "AI Actuary" positioning.

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1. **Entrance (Home)** — Reads "Dr. Rohan Yashraj Gupta — the AI Actuary", sees FIA/FIAI anchor + key stats (years of experience, papers published, talks given) in under a second; credibility hypothesis formed
2. **Selected Works** — Skims placard-style highlights of real, dated work; confirms substance over hype
3. **Speaking & Writing** — Sees past talks with event, audience, and topic context; confirms he can hold a room; taps "Invite Rohan to speak"
4. **The Study (Contact)** — Sends the speaking inquiry through the form; gets warm confirmation that it landed ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

**Persona:** Elena the Event Curator (Primary ⭐)

**Driving Forces Addressed:**
- ✅ **Want:** Instantly verify AI×actuarial credibility; evidence he presents well; a frictionless invite path
- ❌ **Fear:** Booking a hype merchant; wasting an afternoon digging for credentials; dead contact channels

**Business Goal:** Primary Engine (own "AI Actuary") via Objective 1 — 1+ qualified speaking inquiry per quarter
