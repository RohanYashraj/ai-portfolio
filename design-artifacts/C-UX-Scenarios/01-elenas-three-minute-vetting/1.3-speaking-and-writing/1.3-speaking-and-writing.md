## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Prove he can hold a room. Talks listed with event, audience, and topic context; publications alongside. Screenshot-ready credibility signals, and the "Invite Rohan to speak" path — quiet but always visible.

**User Situation:** Elena selected this room from nav; substance confirmed in Selected Works, now verifying stage evidence before shortlisting. She screenshots what convinces her.

**Success Criteria:** Elena finds ≥1 talk matching her audience within 60 seconds; taps "Invite Rohan to speak"; page sections screenshot cleanly (self-contained credibility blocks).

**Entry Points:**
- Room nav from any page · stat-strip talks number on Home · docent deep-link · shared or search links directly to a talk

**Exit Points:**
- "Invite Rohan to speak" → The Study (pre-framed as speaking inquiry) — primary
- Talk/paper entry tap → Archive case detail · DocentPanel
