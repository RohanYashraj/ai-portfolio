## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Confirm substance over hype: a small number (3–5) of recent highlights as placard-style cards — real, dated, one-line results. Work before words. The 15-second proof room.

**User Situation:** Elena scrolled from the hero, hypothesis formed, hunting evidence. Rahul and Ananya pass through here too — it's everyone's second room.

**Success Criteria:** Visitor sees ≥1 dated, verifiable piece within 15 seconds of landing on the site; taps into Speaking & Writing (Elena) or a case detail (Rahul).

**Entry Points:**
- Scroll/room cue from Home (primary) · room nav · docent deep-link

**Exit Points:**
- Room nav → Speaking & Writing (Elena's path)
- Placard tap → Archive case detail (Rahul's path)
- Docent suggested tap → DocentPanel

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Default | 3–5 selected works in CMS | Gallery wall | All |
| Sparse | <3 flagged works | Wall renders what exists — never placeholders | All |
| Reduced-motion | prefers-reduced-motion | Captions static | All |
| Image-pending | Slow network | Placard text renders first; visual lazy-loads with aspect-ratio placeholder | All |

---

## Technical Notes

- "Selected" flag lives in CMS per work item; wall auto-orders newest-first with manual pin override
- Placard text is HTML at build/SSR — the 15-second proof cannot wait for hydration
- Type-in caption animation: IntersectionObserver, one-shot, ≤400ms, disabled on reduced-motion
