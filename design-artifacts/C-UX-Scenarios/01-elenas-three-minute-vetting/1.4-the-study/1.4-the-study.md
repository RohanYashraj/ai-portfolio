## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Convert verified interest into a sent message with zero friction. Short form (pre-framed by arrival intent), honeypot anti-spam, warm confirmation — plus the docent's desk as the conversational alternative. Scenario success happens here. ✓

**User Situation:** Elena arrives via "Invite Rohan to speak" with intent formed — every extra field or ambiguity now risks the inquiry. Others arrive from nav with general contact intent.

**Success Criteria:** Form completes in <60s; submission confirmed warmly with response expectation; zero dead-channel signals. Metric: qualified inquiries (1+ speaking, 2+ consulting/career per quarter).

**Entry Points:**
- "Invite Rohan to speak" (`?intent=speaking`) — pre-framed · room nav ("Contact") · docent hand-off · 404 recovery

**Exit Points:**
- Form submit → confirmation state (terminal — scenario success ✓)
- DocentPanel message path · email fallback link

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Default | Normal load | Form ready, intent frame if param | Fill, submit |
| Pre-framed | `?intent=speaking` | Intent band + Regarding preselected | Fill, submit |
| Validating | Submit with invalid/missing fields | Inline field messages, focus to first error | Correct |
| Sending | POST in flight | Button "Sending…", form locked | Wait |
| Success | 200 response | Form replaced: "Your note is on the desk. Rohan typically replies within two working days." + back-to-gallery link | Return to rooms |
| Error | POST fails | Form intact + "That didn't send — email works: {email}" | Retry, email |
| Spam-caught | Honeypot filled | Renders Success (silent discard) | — |
