# 04: Rahul's Wrong Turn

**Project:** ai-portfolio
**Created:** 2026-07-12
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
Recover from a dead legacy link back into a live gallery room within one tap — the site never confirms the "stale, abandoned" fear, even off the map.

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** Living gallery — zero dead sections, ever
**Objective:** Objective 4 (spirit) — no credibility leak at the weakest touchpoint; legacy rohanyashraj.com URLs will break at relaunch

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** Rahul the Recruiting Lead (Secondary 🚀 — harshest judge of staleness, standing in for all visitors)
**Situation:** Rahul saved a link to a project page on the old rohanyashraj.com months ago; today he opens the stale bookmark to re-check before a hiring panel.

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** The page still exists and the details he remembered are still there.

**Worry:** A generic 404 confirming the site — and the candidate — has gone stale.

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Desktop (office, pre-panel prep)
**Entry:** Opens a months-old bookmark to a legacy URL from the previous site; the path no longer exists.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
Instead of a dead end he gets a gallery-voiced wrong-turn page that names the likely destination and takes him to the live Archive entry in one tap — details recovered in seconds.

**Business Success:**
Zero credibility leak from legacy URLs; the staleness fear is answered by the site's weakest page, reinforcing "living gallery" exactly where template sites fail.

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1. **404 / Fallback** — Sees a gallery-voiced message ("This room has moved") with the site's identity intact, a smart suggestion matching the old URL to its new Archive home, and one-tap paths to the main rooms; taps the suggested link and lands on live content ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

**Driving Forces Addressed:**
- ✅ **Want:** Signal that he's current, not coasting
- ❌ **Fear:** Stale, abandoned site

**Business Goal:** Objective 4 — living gallery, zero dead sections; <15-minute updates keep every destination live
