# 02: Rahul's 55-Second Proof Hunt

**Project:** ai-portfolio
**Created:** 2026-07-12
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
Verify that Rohan's work is real, deep, and current — placard to full case detail in one tap — and decide he earns outreach.

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** Convert visits into consulting & career inquiries
**Objective:** Objective 2 — 2+ qualified consulting/career inquiries per quarter

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** Rahul the Recruiting Lead (Secondary 🚀)
**Situation:** Rahul, 38, Director of Talent at a global insurer, filling a senior actuarial-AI leadership role. He's on his phone between meetings, giving each shortlisted profile under a minute before deciding whether it earns three more.

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** Find a rare, verified actuarial×AI hybrid — shipped work with process and results, not another buzzword bio.

**Worry:** Another template portfolio signalling a commodity candidate, or a stale site that says he's coasting.

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Mobile (this scenario carries the mobile-first mandate)
**Entry:** Taps the portfolio link from Rohan's LinkedIn profile while between meetings, landing on the Entrance, then heads straight for the work.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
Within his 55-second budget he finds the Archive, opens one case detail, sees process, results, and recent dates — enough judgment signal to move Rohan to the "reach out today" pile.

**Business Success:**
A qualified career/consulting inquiry in motion — progress against the 2+/quarter target — from an evaluator who now describes Rohan as "the AI Actuary".

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1. **The Archive (index)** — Arrives via the Entrance's room navigation; filters to Projects; scans dated, typed entries — currency confirmed at a glance
2. **Archive Case Detail** — Opens the strongest match; reads context → approach → results placard structure; verifies real artifacts and judgment ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

**Persona:** Rahul the Recruiting Lead (Secondary 🚀)

**Driving Forces Addressed:**
- ✅ **Want:** Proof of real shipped work in 15 seconds; depth on process & results one tap away; signal he's current
- ❌ **Fear:** Template commodity portfolio; buzzwords without artifacts; stale abandoned site

**Business Goal:** Objective 2 — 2+ qualified consulting/career inquiries per quarter
